About

This study developed a web-based text-to-speech of Akeanon to help children and non-Akeanon speakers to learn the Akeanon language. Akeanon Text-to-Speech System is a web-based system that reads Akeanon text. This study is limited to web platform. It provides an input of text and a speech output. It will have a text box for the text and play button for natural Akeanon sounding synthetic speech.

The contents of the web-based text-to-speech of Akeanon are focuses on speaking Akeanon text with corresponding voice pronunciation. Also Akeanon word list with English meaning to help non-Akeanon readers to understand the word. Also it has a Abakada Alphabet with corresponding sound pronunciations.
